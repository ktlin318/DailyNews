# Source registry

## Priority 1: original sources

- Central banks and statistics agencies: `federalreserve.gov`, `bls.gov`.
- Taiwan regulators and markets: `twse.com.tw`, `tpex.org.tw`, `fsc.gov.tw`, `sfb.gov.tw`, `sitca.org.tw`.
- Company investor relations, newsroom, regulatory filings, and official product pages.
- ETF issuer pages and official prospectuses.

Use these for dates, figures, policy text, product terms, and company statements.

## Priority 2: reporting sources

- Central News Agency, Reuters, Associated Press, and established financial publications.
- Use reporting sources for market reaction, context, and independent coverage.
- For syndicated articles, link to the clearest accessible copy and retain the original publisher.

## Restricted sources

- Do not treat social posts, forums, aggregators, SEO summaries, or AI-generated news pages as factual authorities.
- Do not use search-result snippets as the only evidence.
- Do not bypass paywalls, authentication, robots restrictions, or access controls.
- Treat issuer marketing claims as attributed claims, not independent analysis.

## Selection rule

Prefer one original source plus one independent report for material events. A single accessible original source is acceptable for the MVP but flag material market claims for manual review.
